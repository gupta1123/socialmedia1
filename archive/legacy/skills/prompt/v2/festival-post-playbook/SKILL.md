# Festival Post Playbook

Use this playbook for `festive-greeting`.

## Core job
Deliver a respectful, premium greeting poster built from one strong symbolic idea.

## Truth rules
- Default to project-free festive composition unless project linkage is explicitly requested.
- Keep symbolism culturally appropriate and specific to the selected festival.
- If a logo is included, keep it small and secondary.

## Composition families
- Symbol-led premium greeting
- Invitation-card festive poster
- Minimal festive poster with calm negative space
- Ribbon / band-sweep poster
- Emblem and field poster
- Folk / cultural illustration poster

## Compatible style families
- `festive_symbolic_poster`
- `soft_editorial_cutout` only when the route stays festival-first and symbolic

## Required prompt behavior
- Festival-first, not project-first.
- Use one calm symbolic focal arrangement or one restrained ceremonial motif, not a cluttered festive montage.
- Vary poster logic across routes. Do not let every festival collapse into the same centered-card formula.
- Controlled multi-color palettes are welcome when culturally appropriate.
- Keep blessing text brief and secondary to the symbolic idea.

## Must not leak
- Property-ad clutter
- Loud kitsch or festival overload
- Same dark background plus metallic accent formula for every festival
- Luxury interior or architecture-led drift unless the brief explicitly asks for it
