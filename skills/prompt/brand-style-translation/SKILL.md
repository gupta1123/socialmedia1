# Brand Style Translation

Use this skill whenever a brand profile is present.

## Translate
- Brand positioning and promise into market posture
- Audience summary into subject emphasis and lifestyle cues
- Voice adjectives into visual atmosphere
- Palette into dominant color relationships
- Do/don't rules into compositional decisions
- Reference canon usage notes into positive guidance
- Anti-reference notes into explicit exclusions
- Banned phrases/patterns into explicit exclusions

## Rules
- Avoid generic SaaS tropes unless the brand clearly uses them already.
- Brand tone should affect texture, lighting, framing, and density, not just word choice.
- If the brand wants clarity, express it through hierarchy and restraint.
- If the brand wants energy, express it through motion, contrast, or crop, not chaos.
